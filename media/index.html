Areas of Commentary

What I Cover

My commentary is drawn from direct clinical experience in two of Toronto's highest-acuity hospitals. Every position I take is rooted in what I've seen happen when policy meets the patient.

Nurse Staffing & the Workforce Crisis

How staffing ratios affect patient outcomes, nursing retention, and the long-term sustainability of the healthcare workforce. The gap between what administrators plan and what bedside nurses experience.

Patient Safety in Critical Care

What keeps patients alive in the ICU and what puts them at risk. The clinical realities of monitoring, early intervention, and how systemic failures translate into preventable harm.

Healthcare Burnout & Moral Injury

The psychological cost of working in a system that demands more than it supports. Why nurses leave, what makes them stay, and what recovery looks like for a depleted workforce.

Hospital Policy & Its Bedside Impact

Translating institutional policy into human terms. What budget cuts, restructuring, and administrative decisions look like when they reach the patient room.

Healthcare System Reform

What's broken, what's fixable, and what reform looks like from the perspective of someone who has worked inside the system for close to three decades. Including virtual nursing and provincial float pool models.

Mental Health & Youth Wellbeing

Anxiety, childhood trauma, and the systems that are supposed to help young people but don't. Informed by clinical observation and published work on teen mental health.

Media Bio & Press Kit

Copy the bio below directly into your story attribution. Download the headshot for publication use.

JoAnne Andrea Gomez is a registered nurse, healthcare commentator, and published author based in Toronto, Ontario. With more than 27 years of critical care experience in Trauma-Neuro and Medical-Surgical ICUs at St. Michael's Hospital and Toronto Western Hospital (University Health Network), she provides expert commentary on nurse staffing, patient safety, healthcare burnout, hospital policy, and the nursing workforce crisis. She is the author of "Burnout to Balance: Your 8-Step Guide to Thriving in a Messy Workplace" and "Stronger Than You Think: A Guide for Teens Overcoming Anxiety and Childhood Trauma," and writes regularly on healthcare and workplace issues at medium.com/@msjag416. She is the founder of ProRN Inc., a healthcare consulting company, and holds an active nursing license with the College of Nurses of Ontario. She is available for interviews, expert quotes, op-ed contributions, and panel discussions.
